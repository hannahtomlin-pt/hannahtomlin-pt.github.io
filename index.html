the FFF words: Food, Fitness & Focus

Six week health and fitness programme
Previous Next × Close

Want to get fit or lose weight? Have you thought about going to bootcamp but being in a huge group seems too daunting? Have you thought about Personal Training but that seems too intense or expensive? Do you need some help with your diet and motivation? Yes/yes and yes? Well I have something perfect for you!

Hannah Tomlin – PT brings you the ultimate package; Weekly fitness sessions plus the successful nutritional package that is ‘the FFF word’: six weeks of FOOD, FITNESS & FOCUS. 

FOOD

You’ll be weighed, and measured for your BMI, body fat percentage and muscle percentage at week one and week six.


You’ll receive a personal plan to follow to ensure you hit your goals; this includes; 

• recommended calories 

• your daily macro targets (Carbs/Fats and Protein)

• shopping lists of healthy foods

• example of weekly food menus 

• lots more!

FITNESS

Classes designed to build strength, stamia and burn fat. Variations of all exercises are given and everyone works to their own abilities.  You can expect something different each week; boxing, team challenges and you working against the clock.  


You'll also get weekly exercise homework to complete to help you smash your goals. There is no need for a gym subscription - body weight exercises can be done at home.

FOCUS
You’ll receive guidance, information and support to ensure you stay focused to meet your goals – follow this alongside your new commitment to the workouts and you’ll feel and see the difference!

• mind-set
• preparation
• organisation
• how to detox / tackling toxins
• the effect of hormones

Lets Run

Six week running course - together we are stronger

Everyone can run with the right support!

All courses are physio approved - peace of mind that you are in good hands

Beginners:


For those who have never ran or have very low fitness levels. This is a successful course which aims to develop your confidence, build up stamina and support you on those important first steps! The course is slow paced, with very short amounts of running with lots of walking breaks in-between. We hope, over the course, to build up your running time to enable you to be confident enough to try the joggers if you wish.

Joggers:


A great course for those with experiance of some fitness! You may currently do fitness classes or maybe you used to be a runner. The course starts with a couple of minuites of jogging with short walking inbetween. The course is designed to build strength and stamina - ensuring you can run for longer than your walking breaks! By the end of the course we hope to have you running in around 10 minute bursts.

Progressive C25K

A course for those with a good level of base fitness; you start straight off with 4/5 minute running bursts with short recoveries to catch your breath.  You are supported throughout and work with like-minded individuals. Completing weekly homework will mean you can progresses quickly over the weeks ensuring you can run a 5K in around 35-40 mins by week six.  An accomplishment to be proud of!
The courses are mostly road running and are run by a very supportive coach who has a British Athletics Leadership in running qualification. You will also recieve a physio approved weekly training plan to be done during the week - this includes both strengthing exercises and your running home-work.   Access to a private FB group will ensure your queries are answered promptly.  You'll also recieve support and guidance materials from The Physio Clinic throughout the course.
